## Design Context

### Users
School administrators and teachers managing timetables. Their main goal is to efficiently generate optimized school schedules with teacher and class constraints. The interface should evoke confidence in the tool's reliability and efficiency in handling complex scheduling.

### Brand Personality
Impeccable, premium, maintaining the existing UI flow. The personality is professional yet approachable, emphasizing precision and sophistication in educational tool design.

### Aesthetic Direction
Refined glassmorphism with neumorphic elements, using predefined color palettes (blues, reds, purples) in both light and dark modes. Visual tone is modern and premium, with subtle gradients and shadows creating depth without overwhelming the user.

### Design Principles
1. Maintain visual hierarchy through consistent spacing and typography to guide users through the multi-step process.
2. Use premium materials (glass effects, neumorphic shadows) to convey reliability and sophistication.
3. Ensure responsiveness and accessibility for use in various school environments.
4. Incorporate smooth anCimations to provide feedback and enhance user confidence.
5. Differentiate through intentional asymmetry and varied spacing to avoid generic layouts.
